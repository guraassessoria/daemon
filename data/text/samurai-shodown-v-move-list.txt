System:

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[THE SWORD GAUGE]

The Sword Gauge below the Life Energy Gauge shows attack

strength. When it is low, the amount of damage you give an

opponent will be slight even when you hit them, and when

you hit opponents with the Sword Gauge at MAX, you can

exact extensive damage with a single blow. The Sword Gauge

falls when attacks hit or miss, and subsequently rises as time

passes.

--- page 4 ---
[THE RAGE GAUGE]

The Rage Gauge rises as you take damage from attacks. When

it hits MAX, you can use Knocking-on-the-Sword moves and

try to turn things around with a single blow.

[RAGE EXPLOSIONS]

Activating the "Rage Explosion" enables the use of invincible

attacks that knock back enemy attacks even as you take damage

and the Sword Gauge shoots up to MAX. This lets you use

Knocking-on-the-Sword Methods of Mayhem to get out of

tight spots.

[CONCENTRATION ONE]

"Concentration One" can be activated when the Life Energy

Gauge flashes blue and lets you anticipate your opponent's

moves to aim for a one-blow turnaround. The Rage Gauge

disappears when you use either the "Rage Explosion" or "

Concentration One." The Rage Explosion can get out of a

pinch or you can turn the tide of battle in your favor using

Concentration One-either option greatly changes how you

do battle.
